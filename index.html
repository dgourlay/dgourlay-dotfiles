Welcome to Derek Gourlay's dotfiles

Here you will find dgourlay-dotfiles which are simply my most frequently used dotfiles The repo is arranged as a homesick castle. Installation is easy:

$ gem install homesick
$ homesick clone dgourlay/dgourlay-dotfiles
$ homesick symlink dgourlay/dgourlay-dotfiles

That's it!

Author

You guessed it, dgourlay-dotfiles are authored by none other than Derek Gourlay (@dgourlay). However credit for the content cannot go to myself as they are probably kludges of snippets from all over the net!
